## Canadian Entrepreneurs' Incentive
Draft legislative proposals introduce the Canadian Entrepreneurs' Incentive, which will reduce the taxes on capital gains from the disposition of certain shares of a small business corporation owned directly by an eligible individual. *See Canadian Entrepreneurs' Incentive in the [Income determination](income-determination.html)* *section for more information.*
