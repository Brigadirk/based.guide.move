## Corporate - Branch income
Last reviewed - 03 February 2025

Liechtenstein taxation is imposed on the profit attributable to the branch. The annual corporate minimum tax of CHF 1,800 is also applicable.
There is no withholding tax (WHT) on profit transfer to the head office.
