# Guatemala
## Individual - Tax administration
Last reviewed - 16 January 2025
## Taxable period
The tax year for individuals runs from 1 January to 31 December.
## Tax returns
Taxes on income are governed by the income tax law (_Ley del Impuesto sobre la Renta_) and its related regulations. Administration of the law is vested with the Tax Office of Guatemala (_Superintendencia de Administraci√≥n Tributaria_).
Husbands and wives cannot file joint returns.
## Payment of tax
Income tax must be withheld from salaries; rentals; royalties; fees for technical, professional, or scientific activities; or any kind of individual services.
